How to read this chart: Countries are ranked left-to-right from lowest to highest SDG progress index score. A curve above the line of equality (grey dashed) indicates progressive aid allocation — more aid goes to countries with worse SDG performance relative to their population share. A curve below the line indicates regressive allocation. The Suits Index summarises this as a single number (negative = progressive, positive = regressive, zero = perfectly aligned)

Data: OECD Development Assistance Committee database; AidData (China); UN World Population Prospects; UN SDG Dashboard. Data cover 115 aid-recipient countries, 2015–2023.
